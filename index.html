About Me

🎓
Ihsanulhaq Fadhlurrahman.
Undergraduate English Student & Educational Technology Enthusiast

I am an undergraduate student in English Language Teaching at Universitas Muhammadiyah Surabaya, Indonesia. With a passion for language education and a keen interest in how technology can enhance learning experiences, I created EduLingua to share insights, research, and practical strategies for English language teaching in the digital age.

My academic interest lies at the intersection of linguistics, second language acquisition, and digital tools that can transform how English is taught and learned in Indonesian higher education contexts.
